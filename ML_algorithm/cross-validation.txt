📌 What Cross Validation Actually Does
It:
Splits data into multiple folds (e.g., 5 folds)
Trains model on different subsets
Evaluates on different validation sets
Gives average performance

🎯 Why It Is Important?
🔴 Detect Overfitting
If:
Train accuracy = high
CV score = much lower
→ Overfitting detected

🔵 Detect Underfitting
If:
Train accuracy = low
CV score = also low
→ Model too simple
