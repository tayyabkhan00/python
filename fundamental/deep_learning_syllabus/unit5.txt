Case Study and Applications

1. ImageNet
What is ImageNet?
ImageNet is a very large image dataset used to train and test deep learning models.
Contains 14+ million images
Images are divided into 1000 classes (dog, cat, car, etc.)
Why is ImageNet important?
Made deep learning popular
Used to train famous CNN models:
AlexNet
VGG
ResNet
Helps models learn features like edges, shapes, textures
