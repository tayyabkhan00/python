3D Solar System

This 3D visualization shows our solar system with all planets and their major moons. Each planet orbits the Sun in a tilted elliptical path (shown as circles for simplicity).

Features:
• 3D perspective with depth
• Planet rotation (Earth & Jupiter)
• Saturn's rings in 3D
• Moon orbits around planets
• Interactive camera controls

Use controls to change viewing angle and animation speed.
