Solar System Facts

Our solar system has 8 planets and at least 290 moons. The inner planets (Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars) are rocky, while the outer planets (Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune) are gas giants.

In this visualization, each planet orbits the Sun at different speeds, and their moons orbit around them. Saturn's rings are also represented.

Note: Orbits and sizes are not to scale for visibility.
