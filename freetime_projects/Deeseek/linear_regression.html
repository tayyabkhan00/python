Error Analysis

The residuals (errors) are the vertical distances between data points and the regression line. The regression line minimizes the sum of squared errors (SSE).

Error Visualization

Each red dashed line represents a residual - the vertical distance between a data point and the regression line. Points above the line have positive errors, points below have negative errors.

Interpretation:

  • SSE: Sum of squared residuals. Lower is better.
  • MSE: Average of squared residuals.
  • RMSE: Square root of MSE, in same units as Y.
  • MAE: Average absolute residual, less sensitive to outliers.
  • R²: Proportion of variance explained (0 to 1). Higher is better.
